About me

Hi, my name is Pirooz, an undergraduate student studying computer science and cognitive science at the University of Toronto.

As this is just the start of my career, I am very eager to learn as much as possible in computer science. Over the last year, I have gained many skills in Python, Java and C# ranging from sorting algorithms, software design, and time complexity, and I am quite eager to learn more. Therefore, I am currently learning Javascript and am working hard to improve my programming skills. I am also very devoted to my education and really enjoyed the courses I have completed, currently holding a 4.0 CGPA.

Furthermore, I am constantly seeking opportunities to display my skills while also improving on these skills. I have worked on a few projects in the past few months, including a Discord bot that lets people set reminders for important dates and play Connect 4 with other users or with an AI. Currently, I am focusing on my studies and work at the Kidney Health Education and Research Group. I am also open to employment for the upcoming summer term, so feel free to contact me via email, and I will answer as soon as possible!

Outside of programming, I spend most of my free time practicing boxing, going to the gym, gaming with friends, and playing with my adorable kittens (Check out my Instagram!).
