About me

Hi, my name is Pirooz, an undergraduate student studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science at the University of Toronto.

As this is just the start of my career, I am very eager to learn as much as possible in Computer Science. Over the last year, I have gained many skills in Python and C# ranging from sorting algorithms to time complexity, and I am quite eager to learn more. Therefore, I am currently learning Javascript and COBOL and am working hard to improve my programming skills. I am also very devoted to my education and really enjoyed the courses I have completed, currently holding a 4.0 CGPA.

Furthermore, I am constantly seeking opportunities to display my skills while also improving on these skills. I have worked on a few projects in the past couple of months, including a Discord bot that lets people set reminders for important dates and play Connect 4 with other users or with an AI. On top of this, I am currently adding more features to my Discord bot and improving my BookThink app, hoping to create a better product. I am also open to employment, so feel free to contact me via email, and I will answer as soon as possible!

Other than Computer Science, I love cycling, inline skating, and cooking.
